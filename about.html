About me

Welcome to my personal website!

My name is Rachel Owens and I am an Irish Art Historian based in London. I created this website to bring together my research, to act as a platform where I can share exhibition reviews, personal projects and publications. I love to research and write about art and would love to be able to share this with those who enjoy reading about art. Please reach out to me if you have any feedback about my writings.

I hope you enjoy!
